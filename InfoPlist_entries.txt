──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
INFO.PLIST ENTRIES TO ADD
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

In Xcode, open your project's Info.plist and add these two keys.
The easiest way: click the "+" button next to any existing row.

KEY:   NSCameraUsageDescription
TYPE:  String
VALUE: SubwaySurferMotion needs your front camera to track body pose for gesture control.

KEY:   UIRequiresFullScreen
TYPE:  Boolean
VALUE: YES
(This prevents iPadOS Split View, which can interfere with full-screen camera)

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CAPABILITIES TO ENABLE (in Xcode → your Target → Signing & Capabilities)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

No extra capabilities are needed beyond the default.
Camera access is granted by the NSCameraUsageDescription key above.

If you later want AirPlay / external display, no special entitlement is needed —
iOS handles it automatically via UIScreen notifications.
